Characters

  • 🐡 Panicked Speaker (Pufferfish): Afraid of public speaking. Goal: to learn to express oneself without excessive tension.
  • 🦀 The Fugitive (Crab): Avoids new situations. Goal: to try stepping out of the “shell” and face the world.
  • 🐬 The Clown (Dolphin): Hides true emotions. Goal: to learn to show real feelings and personal traits.
  • 🐟 Invisible (Flounder): Blends into the environment. Goal: to learn to show up and find one’s place among others.
  • 🐍 Writer (Eel): Expresses thoughts only in writing. Goal: to learn to share the inner world directly with others.
